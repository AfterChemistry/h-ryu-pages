Welcome

Haonan Liu is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Chemical Science and Engineering, Institute of Science Tokyo (renamed from Tokyo Institute of Technology). His research focuses on polymer analysis — nanomechanics, high-pressure optics, and high-frequency dielectric behavior.
